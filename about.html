About Bihar

Learn about the cultural richness of Bihar

Overview

Bihar is one of the oldest inhabited places in the world, rich in history, culture, and tradition. It was the center of major empires like the Maurya and Gupta dynasties and home to spiritual traditions like Buddhism and Jainism.

Culture & Heritage

From the ancient ruins of Nalanda University to the sacred Bodh Gaya, Bihar is steeped in religious and architectural legacy. Traditional folk music, Madhubani paintings, Chhath Puja, and regional festivals showcase Bihar’s vibrant cultural identity.

Languages & People

People in Bihar primarily speak Hindi, Bhojpuri, Maithili, and Magahi. They are known for their hospitality, hard work, and resilience.

Famous Places

  • Bodh Gaya – where Buddha attained enlightenment
  • Patna – capital city with rich museums and heritage sites
  • Rajgir – historic and religious destination
  • Vaishali – one of the world's first republics
